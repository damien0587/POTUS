The Facts about Cannibis.

FACTS

Cannabis grown for industry is called industrial marijuana, or hemp. Hemp typically has very low concentrations of THC. Hemp has many uses. The seeds can be crushed for oil, food and beauty products. Hemp fibers can be used for paper, sturdy fabrics and rope. People have grown hemp for millennia. Marijuana is the slang term for portions of the Cannabis plant. It is one of the oldest psychoactive substances used by man. .

MEDICINAL MARIJUANA

Marijuana can help alleviate symptoms for people who suffer from many illnesses and diseases. From alleviating tremors caused by Epilepsy and easing the effects of Tourette's, to alleviating pain caused by cancer and cancer medications. In fact, it's been proven to slow down and kill cancer cells. It assists with neurological damage such as concussions or stroke. It also can stop HIV from spreading, and can slow the progression of Alzheimers. For thousands, Medicinal marijuana has been a life-saver. Prescription medications are only causing worse problems for those that already suffer.

CONSUMER OPTIONS

There are many ways to safely consume marijuana, here are some options: burning like tobacco or vaporizing; Edibles like brownies or cookies,gummy candies, lollipops; lotions, waxes and oils;
